Baking pumpkin pie:

  1. Look up recipe
    1. Through google or cook books
  2. Make sure you got all the necessary ingredients and tools
    1. Make a shopping list if needed for whatever is left over
    2. Go to the store
    3. Buy the crust
    4. Order it from Doordash
  3. Clean area for cooking
  4. Preheat oven to 350 degree F
  5. Mix together ingredients in bowl
    1. Mix dry ingredients
    2. Mix wet ingredients
    3. Mix both together
    4. OPTIONAL make whipped cream
  6. Pour into crust
  7. Bake
